About SlateSafe

The Search for a Better Mount

SlateSafe started because I couldn't find a professional Samsung Tab A9+ enclosure that met the demands of a high-traffic environment. I needed a mount that was tamper-resistant and VESA 75mm compatible, but slim enough to look like part of the architecture, not a bulky afterthought.

Engineered for Purpose

After testing 40+ iterations and various wiring solutions, I developed the ideal package for video conferencing and corporate kiosks. Every SlateSafe product is designed with a focus on:

  • Integrated Functionality: Precision access for cameras and microphones, ensuring clear audio/video for conferencing endpoints.
  • Ultra-Slim Profile: We developed a custom low-profile USB-C cable to eliminate bulk and allow for tight, flush mounting.
  • Versatile Installation: Designed to transition seamlessly between wall mounts and tabletop stands.
  • Fair Pricing: High-end, secure solutions shouldn't cost as much as the tablet itself.
