Our Story

Cost-effective, practical tablet solutions for visitor kiosks, POS systems, and home automation mounts

SlateSafe was born out of frustration. I needed a practical solution for mounting tablets—something low profile, tamper resistant, and able to be installed in a variety of places. The market offerings were either too expensive or didn't quite fit the need. So I decided to create my own.

What started as solving my own problem quickly revealed a bigger opportunity. Whether it's a visitor check-in kiosk, a POS system at a coffee shop, or a home automation tablet mount for Home Assistant—people everywhere need cost-effective tablet solutions that actually work. The existing options came with premium price tags that didn't match the practical needs of most users.

That's where 3D printing changed everything. Using ABS and PETG-CF materials, I could create products that were durable, practical, and most importantly—affordable. Every design decision focuses on what actually matters: low profile installation, tamper resistance, and flexibility in mounting options. No unnecessary features. No inflated costs. Just solutions that work.

Our Mission

To provide cost-effective, practical tablet accessories that solve real problems. From our low profile USB-C flat cable—the best priced and sleekest solution for mounted Samsung tablets—to our tamper-resistant enclosures, we're here to make tablet mounting accessible to everyone.

Our products are perfect for visitor check-in kiosks, POS systems, home automation tablet mounts, and anywhere else you need a reliable, low-profile tablet solution. We maintain quality through our 3D printing process with ABS and PETG-CF materials, and we ship in limited batches to ensure each item receives proper attention.

When you choose SlateSafe, you're getting products designed by someone who faced the same challenges you're facing. Practical solutions. Fair prices. That's our promise.
